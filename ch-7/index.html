Chapter 7 Exercise A

Posted on July 1, 2016 by Mohammad Rashidi

1. Suppose $n$ is a positive integer. Define $T\in\ca L(\mathbb F^n)$ by \[T(z_1,\cdots,z_n)=(0,z_1,\cdots,z_{n-1}).\]Find a formula for $T^*(z_1,\cdots,z_n)$. Solution: By definition, we have \[\begin{align*}\langle (z_1,\cdots,z_n),T^*(w_1,\cdots,w_n)\rangle=&\langle T(z_1,\cdots,z_n),(w_1,\cdots,w_n) \rangle \\=& z_1w_2+\cdots+z_{n-1}w_{n}=\langle (z_1,\cdots,z_n),(w_2,\cdots,w_n,0)\rangle.\end{align*}\]Therefore $T^*(w_1,\cdots,w_n)=(w_2,\cdots,w_n,0)$ or $T^*(z_1,\cdots,z_n)=(z_2,\cdots,z_n,0)$.
